About SCM Concepts

EOQ (Economic Order Quantity):
EOQ calculates the most economical order quantity to minimize total ordering and holding costs.

Reorder Level:
Reorder Level indicates the inventory level at which a new order should be placed.

AI-based Decision Logic:
Simple rule-based logic is used to recommend reorder decisions automatically.
